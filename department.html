Department Information

The Sri Lanka Railway formerly known as the Ceylon Government Railway has an eventful history of 150 years from the day the possibility of having a railway line from Colombo to Kandy and beyond was frist discussed.By 1842 , the necessity of the railway track was identified by the colonial rulers as the road alone was provied inadequate transport for coffee as there were not sufficient bullock carts bullocks available.

Moreover, it then took 12 days for a bullock cart to make the marathon journey of 72 miles from Colombo to Kandy to meet the demand of planters.the railway track to Kandy was compled after 25 years from the time it was frist discussed in 1942.The planters continued to demand extension of the main line beyondperadeniya towards the hill country station to server the fast expanding plantation industry.

The Badulla Railway line was completed in 1924.the line is considered to be one of the finst train jornyes in thw world in terms of enginearing marvel and breathtaking scencer.
